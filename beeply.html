DATA PRIVACY
Beeply Privacy policies Last updated: 09 MAR 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to beeply. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We respect your privacy and don't collect any of your sensitive or non-sensitive data.Your data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up to cloud services like iCloud Backup, GDrive, or similar. Even then, Beeply will never have direct access to your data. Beeply is designed to store all user data locally on the device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal health data on our servers.

App Usage & Crash Reporting

We may collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable or health-related information.

3. Changes to Privacy Policy

Our privacy practices are subject to change. If we decide to modify this policy, we will notify you appropriately before implementing any changes requiring your consent.

4. Ads Targeting Preferences

Since Beeply relies on ads as a source of revenue for improvement and development, we may display advertisements. However, we ensure that users have full control over how ads are displayed, with a focus on personalization.

  • Personalized ads - display personalized ads for better experience
  • Under age of consent - Restrict ads for users under the legal age of concerts by only showing non-personalized ads
  • Child directed consent - Only the non personalized child safe ads are shown
